NIGERIAN ECONOMICS STUDENTS ASSOCIATION

The Nigerian Economics Student Association (NESA), is a formally organised body of students who come together for intellectual, social, career and relational welfare of its members. This association is spread across Nigerian universities but here in Nnamdi Azikiwe Univerisity we have about 500-600 students.

ABOUT US

  1. OUR RESEARCH INSTITUTE:

    In pursuit of academic excellence and human capital developement, the Policy Analysis and Research Forum(PAREF) is equiping young Nigerian economics students with the art of research in related career path by creating a facilitating environment which gives students the platform to make and present their research finding under the auspice of experts in realated area of research discipline

  2. OUR FELLOWSHIP:

    Students sometimes faces some mental, psyhological and spiritual problems which is deterrant to the healthy academic and social lifestyle. As a means of consolidating the mind of our students, the National Fellowship of Economics Student(NAFECS) will provide spiritual, mental and psychological aid to students with related concerns.
